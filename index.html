FRANK OCEAN



History

Born in 1987, Christopher Edwin Breaux grew up in New Orleans with his mother and Grandmother. Grew up listening to jazz and R&B and learned music in a studio.. In 2005, Hurricane Katrina destroyed his home amd studio leading him to move to Los Angeles.


He started working as a ghostwriter writing for many artist like Justin Bieber, John legend and Brandy. He then joined the hip hop collective Odd Future in 2009. In 2011, he released his first mixtape

His first breakthrough "Ultra Nostalgia" was widely acclaimed and what made him famous. His second album "Channel Orange" was released in 2012 and won him a Grammy for best urban contemporary album. In 2016, he released his third album "Blonde" which was also widely acclaimed and topped the charts. He has also realed another album "Endless" in 2016 but it can only be found on Apple Music.
