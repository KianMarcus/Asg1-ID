Bob Marley Website 

This project is a website for the singer Bob Marley. It presents his music, merchandise and general overview of what his music is and his history. It attracts viewers with its pictures and shows them the life of Bob Marley.

Feautures include:
-Tour Dates
-Album Covers and titles
-Merchandise



Design Process
This website was made for anyone interested in learning more about Bob Marley and his music. It is super simple and easy to read and displays everything you need to know about Bob Marley.

As a new listener, I wanna see more of his songs so I can go to the music page and look at all the albums and songs listed.

As a fan, I wanna see his merchandise so I can support him so I go over to the merch page and take a look.
